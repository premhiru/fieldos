## Brand Idea

FieldOS is the operating layer for work that happens beyond the desk. Its identity communicates structured operations, visible status, and decisive follow-through. The brand should feel appropriate in an airport operations center, infrastructure program office, or field coordination room.

## Identity System

The FieldOS mark is a compact field grid forming an `F`, finished by a green status signal. The structure represents organized field information; the signal represents an operation in a known, healthy state.

- **Primary lockup:** mark, `FieldOS` wordmark, and optional `Field operations` descriptor.
- **Compact lockup:** mark and wordmark for mobile headers.
- **App icon and favicon:** standalone mark on graphite.
- **Loading mark:** the mark beside neutral skeleton structures; do not spin or distort the logo.
- **Clear space:** preserve at least one quarter of the mark width on all sides.
- **Minimum size:** 24 px digital. Use the simplified standalone mark below 32 px.

## Typography

FieldOS uses one sans-serif family: Inter, with the platform sans-serif stack as a resilient fallback. Weights are limited to regular, medium, and semibold. Display typography is reserved for authentication and true first-impression moments; operational screens use compact headings.

## Color

The product is approximately 90 percent neutral grayscale. Graphite establishes authority without making blue the product identity.

| Role     | Use                                      | Reference |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------- |
| Graphite | Primary actions and the identity mark    | `#181c20` |
| Green    | Healthy, connected, complete             | `#1f9d61` |
| Amber    | Attention, review, approaching risk      | `#d18b16` |
| Red      | Critical, destructive, failed            | `#ce3e45` |
| Blue     | Informational, selected, linked evidence | `#2878c7` |

Color must always be paired with text, an icon, or a structural cue when it communicates status.

## Illustration

Empty-state illustrations use thin operational frames, one familiar Lucide icon, neutral borders, and no decorative scenes. They should suggest an organized workspace waiting for data. Do not use mascots, stock photography, AI motifs, gradients, or abstract decoration.

## Voice

FieldOS is direct, calm, and useful. State what happened, why it matters, and what the user can do. Prefer `No conversations found` over system implementation language. Avoid hype, excessive reassurance, and anthropomorphizing AI.

## Usage Rules

- Do not recolor the mark with feature colors.
- Do not stretch, rotate, outline, or place the mark in another container.
- Do not use blue as a dominant background or decorative accent.
- Do not pair the wordmark with a second display typeface.
- Do not use the green signal for decorative emphasis; it retains operational meaning.
