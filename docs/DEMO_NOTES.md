## Presentation

FieldOS should be presented as an operations command center, not as a collection of AI features. Begin with the live operational picture, move from a field message to evidence and a recommendation, then show the human approval step and resulting project timeline.

## Suggested Flow

1. Log in and introduce the calm operations workspace.
2. Review dashboard attention signals and one recommendation.
3. Open the related project and inspect timeline and evidence.
4. Open Inbox to show the originating field conversation.
5. Approve or complete one user-authorized action.
6. Close with the grounded project report and source evidence.

## Visual Checks

- Use a desktop viewport wide enough to show the persistent navigation.
- Keep browser zoom at 100 percent.
- Confirm the active workspace contains seeded demo data before the session.
- Avoid showing settings, connection internals, or operations health unless the audience asks.
- Dark mode is supported through the operating-system preference but the standard demo uses light mode.
